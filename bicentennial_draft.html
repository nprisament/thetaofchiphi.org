About The Event

Join us for the bicentennial celebration of the Chi Phi Fraternity in the historic city of Troy, New York, hosted by the Theta Alumni of the Chi Phi Fraternity. A formal invitation with further details, including an RSVP and comprehensive schedule of events, will be forthcoming.

Banquet Venue

Formal Banquet location info and gallery

Hilton Garden Inn Troy

Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute is under five minutes' drive from our hotel. It's a quick trip to downtown Troy and I-787. Albany International Airport and the Rensselaer Amtrak Station are both within 10 miles. Grab a bite at our restaurants, Recovery Sports Grill and Garden Grille & Bar. Enjoy our indoor pool and free WiFi.
