About The Bicentennial Celebration

About The Event

Join us for the Bicentennial Celebration of the Chi Phi Fraternity in the historic city of Troy, New York, hosted by the Theta Alumni of the Chi Phi Fraternity.

Where

The Chi
1981 15th St, Troy, New York

Friday to Sunday
October 4th-6th 2024

The formal banquet dinner for the celebration will be taking place on Saturday evening at the Hilton Garden Inn on Hoosick Street in Troy, New York.

Below, you will find a full schedule of the weekend's festivities, with specific venue and activity information.

Event Schedule

  • Full Schedule

Friday 10/6, 6PM - 10PM

Dinner and Reception @ Wolff's Biergarten in Albany, NY

Google Maps Link This event is 21+.

Step into an evening of fun and camaraderie at a favorite venue of Chapter alumnae.

A cash bar will be available and at 10pm the venue will open its doors to the public.

Saturday 10/7, 1PM - 3PM

Bowling @ Uncle Sam Lanes in Downtown Troy, NY

Google Maps Link

Get ready for bowling at Uncle Sam Lanes in the heart of Downtown Troy. Alumni are encouraged to reconnect with other alumni by getting breakfast/brunch or going to the Troy Farmer's Market prior to the event.

A cash bar will be available throughout the event.

Saturday 10/7, 5PM - 10PM

Semi-Formal Banquet @ Hilton Garden Inn

Venue

This event will begin with a cocktail reception featuring butler-passed hors d'oeuvres! Then join us for a momentous evening as we celebrate 200 years of brotherhood and academic excellence at the Bicentennial Banquet, honoring the legacy of the Chi Phi Fraternity and Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Hosted in the illustrious Ferris Grand Ballroom, this banquet will be a night to remember.

The menu will include a soup dejour, garden salad and freshly baked bread to start. The entrees will be Chicken Francaise, Sliced Roast Beef Tenderloin (GF), and a Pasta Primavera (DF / V). Accompanying these dishes are sides of roasted asparagus and roasted garlic mashed potatoes. To end on a sweet note, desserts include classic New York Style cheesecake with berries and whipped cream, as well as a decadent Triple Chocolate Mousse Cake. Throughout the evening, a cash bar will offer both imported and domestic drinks.

The celebration will also feature speeches from the Theta Alumni Association, reflecting on the rich history and enduring legacy of Chi Phi and RPI. The Theta Housing Corporation will also kick off their capital campaign.

This event will be semi-formal / pin attire.

Sunday 10/8, 11AM - 4PM

Alumni Cookout and Field Day @ The Chi, 1981 15th Street

Google Maps Link

To round out the weekend, we will finish with a cookout and field day at our Chapter Houses. Our Brothers will be grilling food for everyone while we reminisce on the weekend and play lawn games.

F.A.Q.

  • How do I RSVP/Register?

    The RSVP/Registration is live now! Go buy your ticket(s) and register at the link below!

  • Can I bring my significant other?

    Yes, please do, the more the merrier!

  • Can I bring a friend as a +1 instead?

    Yes, the more the merrier!

  • Can I bring my children?

    Yes, you are welcome to bring your family. Please note that the Friday night event is 21+. Please buy a ticket for all attendees unless otherwise approved by the planning committee.

  • Do I need to buy a second ticket for my +1?

    Yes, all attendees must register and pay the registration cost.

  • Can I visit the Chapter House on Friday and Saturday?

    Yes, active Brothers will be available to welcome you in to visit the house.

  • Can I come if I am not a Theta Chapter Brother?

    Yes, all Chi Phi's are invited to join us!

  • Is there a golf event?

    A golf event is being considered for Friday or Saturday. If this event is on Saturday, it would take place concurrently with bowling and take the place of bowling for those participating. This event would have an additional cost and will be planned tentatively based on interest at the time of registration.

  • What is Chi Phi National doing to celebrate the Bicentennial?

    This event is an officially registered celebration with Chi Phi National. Chi Phi National also has a series of additional regional events and special promotions you can find at their bicentennial website.

  • How can I learn more about what the Active Chapter has been up to?

    Check out the newsletter and the Chapter Social media. For more details email the bicentennial committee or the Active Chapter Gamma at gamma.theta.chiphi@gmail.com.

  • Where can I learn more about the Housing Corporation Capital Campaign?

    Information about the campaign will be anounced at the banquet.
